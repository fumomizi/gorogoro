🏔️ Welcome to the Peaks of Yokai Mountain 🏔️

The Gorogoro Shogi Dojo

In the shadowed valleys and misty peaks of Yokai Mountain, where ancient spirits dwell and mystical energies flow, lies a battleground of intellect and strategy. Here, the tengu, masters of the winds and keepers of secrets, challenge all who dare to a duel of gorogoro Shogi.

🎴 The Challenge of the Tengu 🎴

Do you possess the cunning and wisdom to face the tengu in a match of gorogoro Shogi? In this hallowed dojo, where the board is set in a sacred 5×6 grid, challengers must prove their mettle by winning more than 15 games out of 30 against these mythical beings.

The tengu await, their clothes rustling and eyes gleaming, ready to engage in a battle of wits and tactics. Each victory brings honor; each defeat, a lesson.

🍃 Rise to the Challenge: The Path of the Gorogoro Shogi Warrior 🍃

Embrace the spirit of the mountain, hone your skills, and immerse yourself in a game that transcends the mundane. Whether you're a seasoned Shogi player or a curious wanderer stepping onto the enchanted path, the gates of Yokai Mountain are open to you.

Challenge the tengu, learn their secrets, and perhaps, become one of them. The wind whispers of glory and adventure, and the game is set.

🎏 Begin Your Journey 🎏

Enter the dojo, take your place at the board, and let the games of gorogoro Shogi commence. The tengu are ready. Are you?
