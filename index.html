📜 Rules 📜

A 5×6 version with 16 pieces is known as goro goro dōbutsu shōgi (ごろごろどうぶつしょうぎ), which is the same as goro goro Shogi. <...>

Promotion zones are the first and last 2 ranks. It has the same restrictions on dropped pieces in shogi including two pawns (nifu) and pawn drop with immediate checkmate (uchifuzume). Perpetual check (4 move repetition) is also a draw as in dōbutsu shōgi.
